Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Overview

Video Poster is a desktop app used to generate short-form videos from local folders and optional account settings. This policy explains what information the app may store and how it is used.

Information Stored

The app may store selected local folder paths, video templates, text styling, schedule settings, output folder settings, and optional TikTok account/API fields such as client key, client secret, access token, refresh token, open ID, caption, and privacy preference.

Local Storage

Settings are stored locally on the user's computer. The app does not operate a hosted database for these settings.

Use of TikTok Information

If TikTok account/API fields are entered, they are intended to be used only for connecting to TikTok posting features requested by the user. The app should only access or publish content when the user configures and enables that workflow.

Content Files

The app reads local audio and video files selected by the user to generate output videos. These files remain on the user's computer unless the user chooses to upload or publish generated videos through a connected service.

Third-Party Services

If TikTok posting is enabled, use of TikTok services is subject to TikTok's own terms and privacy policy.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app owner through the GitHub repository where this page is hosted.
